sort1 uses: Bubble Sort

How do you know?: sorted50000 - no delay, random & reversed50000 - long delay:
optimised bubble sort, sorted50000 - omega(N) (faster), random & reversed50000 - theta(N^2) and O(N^2) respectively (slower).

sort2 uses: Merge Sort

How do you know?: random, reversed & sorted50000 - no delay:
random, reversed & sorted50000 - theta(NlogN), O(NlogN) & omega(NlogN) respectively (similar speed, faster than the N^2 in other sorts)

sort3 uses: Selection Sort

How do you know?: random, reversed & sorted50000 - long delay:
random, reversed & sorted50000 - theta(N^2), O(N^2) & omega(N^2) respectively (similar speed, slower than sort2)
